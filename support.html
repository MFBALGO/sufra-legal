Common questions

I didn’t receive my sign-in code.
Check that you entered your email address correctly, then request the code again, and look in your spam folder. If it still doesn’t arrive, email us.
How does the cook see the menu?
Open Sufra on a tablet in the kitchen, choose the cook role, and enter the pairing code (or scan the QR) from the planner’s “Set up kitchen display” screen. The day’s menu appears in large type and updates within seconds of any change.
How do I delete my account and data?
In the app, go to More → Delete account to permanently delete your account and all associated data. You can also email us at the address above and we will delete it for you.
